1. Who We Are

TechJedi LLP ("TechJedi", "we", "our", or "us") is a Limited Liability Partnership registered in India, providing WhatsApp Business API messaging solutions to businesses.

Registered Business: TechJedi LLP
Country: India
Contact: contact@techjedi.in

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our platform and services.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Business contact information: name, email address, phone number, and business name when you register or contact us.
  • WhatsApp contact data: phone numbers and names of your customers/contacts that you upload to our platform for messaging.
  • Message content: the text content of messages you create and send through our platform.
  • Account credentials: username and securely hashed password for your admin account.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps, and referring URLs.
  • Message delivery data: sent, delivered, read, and failed status updates received via Meta webhook callbacks.
  • Usage data: features used, messages sent, campaign statistics.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment card information directly (handled by third-party payment processors if applicable).
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data such as Aadhaar numbers, medical information, or biometric data.
  • We do not read or store the content of incoming WhatsApp messages from end users.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our WhatsApp messaging platform
  • Send WhatsApp messages on your behalf to your opted-in contacts
  • Track message delivery status and generate reports
  • Process your instructions and respond to your requests
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our platform based on usage patterns

4. WhatsApp Business API & Data

Our platform is built on the Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API. When you use our platform to send messages:

  • Phone numbers and message content are transmitted to Meta Platforms Inc. to deliver messages.
  • Meta's own WhatsApp Privacy Policy and WhatsApp Business Policy apply to message transmission.
  • We receive delivery status webhooks (sent, delivered, read) from Meta and store them in our database to provide you with analytics.
  • You are responsible for ensuring your contacts have opted in to receive WhatsApp messages from you before adding them to our platform.
Opt-In Requirement: You must only send messages to individuals who have explicitly opted in to receive WhatsApp communications from your business. You must maintain records of opt-in consent. Sending unsolicited messages violates WhatsApp's policy and Indian telecom regulations.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Meta Platforms Inc.: Phone numbers and message content are shared with Meta solely to deliver WhatsApp messages as instructed by you.
  • Service providers: We may share data with hosting providers, database services, and analytics tools that help us operate our platform, all bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority in India.
  • Business transfers: If TechJedi LLP merges or is acquired, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Contact lists: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Message logs: Retained for 12 months, then automatically deleted
  • Account data: Retained for 30 days after account closure, then permanently deleted
  • Legal hold: Some data may be retained longer if required for legal compliance

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at contact@techjedi.in.

7. Your Rights

Under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time
  • Grievance: Lodge a complaint with us or the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: contact@techjedi.in

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
  • API access is protected by JWT tokens with expiry
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of our systems and code

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you in the event of a data breach as required by applicable law.

9. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies: To keep you logged in to the admin panel (essential, cannot be disabled)
  • No tracking or advertising cookies are used on this website

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
